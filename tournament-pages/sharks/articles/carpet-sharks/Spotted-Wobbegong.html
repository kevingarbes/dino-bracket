Spotted Wobbegong

Spotted wobbegongs are master camouflage artists with ornate patterns of spots and blotches that perfectly mimic the light patterns on the seafloor, making them nearly invisible to both prey and predators. Their flattened bodies are adorned with elaborate dermal lobes around the head that break up their outline, while they patiently wait for unsuspecting fish to swim close enough for their lightning-fast ambush attack.
