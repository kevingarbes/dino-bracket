Rusty Carpetshark

Rusty carpetsharks are named for their reddish-brown coloration that helps them blend seamlessly with the algae-covered rocky reefs they inhabit along Australia's coastline. These small, secretive sharks are primarily nocturnal and spend their days hidden in caves or under ledges, emerging at night to use their specialized teeth for crushing the hard shells of crustaceans and mollusks.
