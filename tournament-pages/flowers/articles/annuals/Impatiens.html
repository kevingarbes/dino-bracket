Flower Overview

Beloved for shady gardens, impatiens produce masses of cheerful blooms in reds, pinks, and whites even in low light. Impatiens earned the nickname “Busy Lizzie” for their constant flowering, and their ripe seed pods burst open at a touch—hence the Latin name meaning “impatient.”
