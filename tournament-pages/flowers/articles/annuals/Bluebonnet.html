Flower Overview

Texas bluebonnet (Lupinus texensis) is a lupine wildflower with vivid blue pea-like blossoms that blanket Texas fields every spring. Chosen as the Texas state flower, bluebonnets were widely seeded along highways by Lady Bird Johnson’s programs, and many Texans take annual family photos in the iconic bluebonnet blooms.
