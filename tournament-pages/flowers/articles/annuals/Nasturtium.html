Flower Overview

Nasturtiums are fast-growing annual vines or mounding plants with round leaves and funnel-shaped flowers in bold reds, oranges and yellows. Beyond their looks, they are edible flowers - both leaves and blossoms have a peppery taste and are often added to salads, carrying a dose of Vitamin C and acting as a natural pest trap in the veggie garden.
