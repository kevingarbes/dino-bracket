Flower Overview

The California poppy (Eschscholzia californica) is a drought-tolerant wildflower with silky orange blooms that open under the sun. As the state flower of California, it famously carpets hillsides each spring—during “superbloom” years, countless California poppies turn entire landscapes brilliant orange, a natural spectacle celebrated each April 6 on California Poppy Day.
