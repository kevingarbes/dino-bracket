Flower Overview

Pansies are cool-season annuals known for their “faces” and cold tolerance, often brightening spring and fall gardens when few other flowers bloom. The name “pansy” comes from the French pensée (“thought”), and in the language of flowers pansies symbolize remembrance and free thinking.
