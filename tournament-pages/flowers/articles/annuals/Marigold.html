Flower Overview

Marigolds (genus Tagetes) produce blooms all summer with virtually no care, and their pungent scent even repels many garden pests. In Mexico, marigold flowers (cempasúchil) are famously used on Day of the Dead altars—their bright orange color and musky fragrance are believed to help lead ancestral spirits back.
