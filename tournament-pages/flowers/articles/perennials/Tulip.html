Flower Overview

Tulips are spring-blooming bulb perennials that paint gardens with bold cups of color. Originating in Central Asia and famously prized in Holland, tulips once sparked “Tulipmania” in the 17th century when rare bulbs became as valuable as houses. While modern tulips are often treated as annuals, many varieties will return for several years if winters are cold enough.
