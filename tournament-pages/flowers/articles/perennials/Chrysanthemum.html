Chrysanthemum

Garden chrysanthemums (“mums”) are the quintessential fall flower, blooming in late summer and autumn with dense cushions of yellow, orange, purple, or white. First cultivated in China over 2,500 years ago, mums were said to have such life-giving properties that an ancient Chinese city was named Ju-Xian, meaning “Chrysanthemum City.” They’re also the birth flower for November, symbolizing optimism and joy.
