Flower Overview

Daffodils (Narcissus) are hardy spring perennials known for their bright yellow trumpets that herald the end of winter. They contain a natural chemical (lycorine) that makes them deer and rodent resistant, and according to legend, the first daffodil sprang from the vanity of the Greek youth Narcissus—giving the flower its botanical name and association with rebirth and new beginnings.
