Flower Overview

Butterfly milkweed (Asclepias tuberosa) is a bright orange native perennial and a vital host plant for monarch butterflies, whose caterpillars feed exclusively on milkweeds?. Unlike the milky sap of other milkweeds, this species lacks the latex sap (hence the name “butterfly weed”), making it easier to handle. Its blooms are not only beautiful but also act as a lifesaving nursery for monarchs—planting it helps support the iconic butterfly’s life cycle.
