Breed Overview

Weimaraners are elegant, athletic sporting dogs with distinctive silver-gray coats and striking amber or blue-gray eyes that have earned them the nickname Gray Ghost. Their intense devotion to their people can manifest as velcro-like attachment and occasional separation anxiety, requiring owners who can provide both substantial exercise for their boundless energy and consistent companionship for their people-oriented nature.
