Breed Overview

Golden Retrievers are intelligent, friendly dogs with lustrous golden coats and a gentle, eager-to-please disposition that makes them exceptional family pets and working companions. Their natural affinity for people and remarkable patience have made them ideal service dogs, therapy animals, and hunting partners, though their people-oriented nature means they thrive with human interaction and can develop separation anxiety if left alone too frequently.
