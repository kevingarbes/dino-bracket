Breed Overview

Poodles are highly intelligent, elegant dogs with distinctive curly coats that come in standard, miniature, and toy sizes. Despite their refined appearance, they were originally bred as water retrievers and excel in various dog sports, combining athletic ability with exceptional trainability and hypoallergenic qualities.
