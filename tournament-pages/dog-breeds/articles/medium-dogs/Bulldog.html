Breed Overview

Bulldogs are muscular, medium-sized dogs with characteristic pushed-in faces, loose skin, and an easygoing temperament that makes them excellent family companions. Their distinctive wrinkled appearance and waddling gait hide a gentle, patient nature that typically bonds strongly with children, though their brachycephalic features require owners to be attentive to heat sensitivity and breathing issues.
