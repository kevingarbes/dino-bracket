Breed Overview

Dalmatians are distinctive, athletic dogs with unique spotted coats and a noble heritage as coach dogs trained to run alongside horse-drawn carriages. Their endurance and energy levels remain high into adulthood, requiring significant exercise and training to channel their intelligence and stamina positively, making them best suited for active households rather than sedentary environments.
