Breed Overview

Pugs are charming, compact dogs with distinctive wrinkled faces, curled tails, and a mischievous expression that perfectly matches their playful personality. Their sturdy build and even-tempered nature make them adaptable family companions, though their brachycephalic (flat-faced) structure requires careful attention to exercise and temperature conditions.
