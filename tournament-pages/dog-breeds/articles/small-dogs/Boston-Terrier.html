Breed Overview

Boston Terriers are compact, muscular dogs with distinctive tuxedo-like markings and friendly, bright expressions that earned them the nickname American Gentleman. Their gentle, affectionate temperament combines with moderate exercise needs to make them adaptable companions for various living situations, from apartments to family homes.
