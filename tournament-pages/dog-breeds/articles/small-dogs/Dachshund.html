Breed Overview

Dachshunds (aka Doxens and Weiner Dogs) are clever, curious dogs with long bodies, short legs, and an independent spirit originally bred for hunting badgers. Their courageous personality often comes packaged in three coat varieties: smooth, wirehaired, or longhaired, and they tend to be devoted to their families despite their sometimes stubborn nature.
